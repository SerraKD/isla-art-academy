About Us

Learn how to unleash your inner artist with our painting lessons! At Isla Art Academy, we believe that everyone can draw and paint. We offer classes to beginners and advanced students of all ages using a combination of traditional art education and creative methods. With us, you will learn to express yourself through art while having a great time with amazing people.


We approach classes in a fun way rather than academically. Our main goal is to make everyone love painting, no matter their knowledge or experience. Classes are in mixed groups of ten people and an hour long. The tutor takes care of every student separately to consider their needs. You can pick the materials you want to work with and contact us if you need assistance preparing the essential equipment. We are a pet-welcoming. Bring your little friends as a muse!


Join us to develop your painting skills, have lots of fun, and be part of this great experience! You can sign up below, call us, or visit us.
